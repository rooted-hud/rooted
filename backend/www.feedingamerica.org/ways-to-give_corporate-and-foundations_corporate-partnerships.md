# Cause Marketing and Corporate Partnerships

We are so grateful for your interest in supporting Feeding America. Our Corporate Donors and Partners provide significant resources that allow Feeding America to achieve our vision of a hunger-free America. We are actively seeking multi-faceted partnerships to help us succeed in our mission to increase equitable access to nutritious food for all.

## Corporate Donations

Feeding America accepts donations at all levels. Your contribution will support our efforts to end [food insecurity](https://www.feedingamerica.org/hunger-in-america/food-insecurity) and support the [Feeding America network](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/food-bank-network).

[**Reach out today**](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/corporate-and-foundations/partnership-inquiry) **if you are interested in making a corporate donation to Feeding America.**

## National Corporate Partnerships and Cause Marketing

The significant investment and sponsorship opportunities Corporate Partners provide are essential to ending hunger in America.

Feeding America builds multi-faceted partnerships that include strategic investment, cause marketing activations, or [employee engagement opportunities](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/workplace-giving).   
**Check out** [**our current partners**](https://www.feedingamerica.org/partners) **and** [**current promotions**](https://www.feedingamerica.org/partners/current-promotions)**.**

Feeding America uses the following criteria to evaluate all National Corporate Partnership opportunities:

* National Partnerships require a commitment of six to seven figures on an annual basis
* Requires that the company has been in business for at least one year
* Feeding America will not partner with the following industries:
  + Cannabis/CBD
  + Adult
  + Tobacco
  + Dangerous products/firearms
  + Gambling

Due to federal regulations, we do not accept donations from companies within the marijuana and CBD industry. However, we encourage those industries to check with [your local food bank](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank) about gifts.

Feeding America adheres to the [Better Business Bureau’s Standards for Charity Accountability](https://www.give.org/charity-landing-page/bbb-standards-for-charity-accountability?id=236646).
