# USDA Freezer Warehouse Specialist

Food Bank

[Food Bank of the Rockies](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank/food-bank-of-the-rockies)

City

Aurora

State

Colorado

Post date

April 17, 2026

Description

The USDA Freezer Warehouse Specialist is responsible for storing, staging, and packing food products within freezer environments while supporting United States Department of Agriculture (USDA) program requirements. This role works closely with the USDA warehouse and distribution teams to pull, prepare, and distribute government food orders to partner agencies, ensuring compliance, accuracy, and efficiency in a cold storage setting.

End date

April 23, 2026

Opportunity type

Job

Category

Warehouse / Supply Chain / Drivers

Job responsibilities

• Store, stage, and pack food products within freezer units (Main and Staging)  
• Pull and prepare USDA orders accurately and in a timely manner based on priority  
• Maintain full pick locations, including restacking and reorganizing product for maximum efficiency  
• Verify all orders for accuracy and correct discrepancies as needed  
• Restock product following USDA and food safety storage guidelines  
• Load and unload inbound/outbound shipments safely; move product to appropriate freezer storage locations

Application qualifications

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:  
• Strong commitment to organizational mission and ability to represent the organization professionally  
• Ability to work effectively in a fast-paced, temperature-controlled (freezer) environment  
• Strong attention to detail, especially related to order accuracy and inventory integrity  
• Effective problem-solving, time management, and organizational skills  
• Ability to prioritize multiple tasks and meet deadlines consistently  
• Comfortable working both independently and within a team environment  
• Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to interact professionally with staff and volunteers  
• Basic computer skills and ability to learn warehouse management systems (Ceres ERP preferred)

Required Qualifications:  
• High School diploma or equivalent required  
• Prior warehouse or order fulfillment experience preferred  
• Experience working in cold storage or freezer environments preferred  
• Forklift certification preferred (or ability to obtain)  
• Experience with Microsoft Office (Word, Excel, Outlook) and warehouse systems a plus

About the organization

As the food bank with the largest service area in the contiguous United States, Food Bank of the Rockies is passionate about ensuring every community member has the resources they need to thrive. Supporting our Colorado and Wyoming communities begins with passionate, mission-driven individuals.

The Food Bank’s team is dedicated to creating an efficient food distribution network to reduce and help end hunger across the state. Staff are committed to embodying the Food Bank’s organizational values and mission in all activities.

If you are looking for a mission-focused organization where innovation and teamwork are encouraged and new ideas are valued, then this is an ideal position for you.

The mission of Food Bank of the Rockies is to ignite the power of community to nourish people facing hunger. We provide food and necessities to people in need through signature programs and by teaming up with hundreds of Hunger Relief Partners to serve communities across Colorado and Wyoming. We believe that for a community to thrive, every person must have the resources they need to flourish, and we strive to provide proper nourishment for every neighbor.

Additional information

Work Environment:  
The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.  
• Extended periods working in freezer conditions (approximately -5°F)  
• Standing and walking for long periods; intermittent sitting  
• Frequent use of warehouse equipment including forklifts and pallet jacks  
• Ability to lift and move up to 50 lbs. regularly  
• Ability to push/pull pallets up to 2,000 lbs. using manual or powered equipment  
• Exposure to varying warehouse temperatures (freezer, cooler, ambient)  
• Requires visual accuracy and attention to detail in a fast-paced environment

Expected Hours of Work:  
This is a full-time, non-exempt position working approximately 40 hours per week. Schedules may vary and include weekdays, evenings, and occasional Saturdays. Working from 7:00 am – 3:30 pm.
