# Donors, Food and Fund Partners

## Thank you, Feeding America Donors, Food and Fund Partners!

Many corporations, foundations, and individuals give generously of their time, significant hunger-relief investments, and food and grocery products to help provide food assistance to the tens of millions of people facing hunger in America. In 2023, 48 million million people, including more than 14 million million children, experienced food insecurity, with hunger impacting every community in the country. Feeding America proudly thanks and recognizes the extraordinary commitment of individuals and organizations that are making a meaningful difference in the lives of people facing hunger. Together, we are working to ensure everyone has access to the food they need to thrive. 

### [Become a National Partner](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/corporate-and-foundations/partnership-inquiry "Mission Partners")

Our donors and partners help to provide meals to more than 40 million Americans each year. Learn more on how you or your business could get involved. [Partner with Us ›](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/corporate-and-foundations/partnership-inquiry "Mission Partners")
