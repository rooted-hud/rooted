# Give Stocks and Funds

At Feeding America, we rely on donations to help make food available to families struggling with hunger, and we can’t do this work without you. Every dollar you give can provide at least 10 meals to families in need through the Feeding America network of food banks.

Donating stock, securities, and mutual fund shares is an effective way to help Feeding America fight hunger. Making this gift is simple and offers you valuable financial benefits:

* You can donate appreciated stocks, bonds or mutual funds
* The total value of the stock upon transfer is tax-deductible
* There is no obligation to pay any capital gains taxes on the appreciation

## How To Donate Stocks and Funds
