# Free food and resources

## Free food and resources

Whenever you need it, we have the resources to assist you in bringing nutritious meals – and more – home. Check out the programs available in the Feeding America network:

[![Adriana in front of shelves of fruits and vegetables.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2025-11/Adriana%20400X200.jpg?h=1f38fdba&itok=gp7rLtrA)

### Food distributions

Food distributions offer free food and groceries to individuals and families. They provide enough food to last 2 to 3 days.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank)

[![People standing next to food boxes and a tent.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2024-09/IMG_4326.jpg?h=4a7d1ed4&itok=3Q2EhQYk)

### Mobile pantries

Mobile pantries come to your community. They bring free food and groceries right to your neighborhood. This is a great option if you live where food pantries and stores are hard to find.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/mobile-food-pantry-program)

[![Juana and her daughter hugging each other.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2025-09/Juana%20400X200.jpg?h=1f38fdba&itok=VpRCX-LW)

### SNAP application assistance

If you need extra help buying groceries, you may qualify for SNAP. Connect with your local food bank and learn how to apply.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/snap)

[![Pastor Dennis carrying a box of food outside of a church.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2025-12/IMG_9662.jpg?h=1f38fdba&itok=PtTbIPMa)

### Senior food programs

If you’re a senior, many programs are available for free meals and groceries, including senior food boxes and home delivery.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/senior-programs)

## Free food for kids

There are many programs to help children and teens have food before, during and after school.

[![Backpacks filled with canned food and school supplies.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2023-11/20220330_FeedingAmerica_SanAntonio-178-400x200.jpg?h=4a7d1ed4&itok=-rC14FUy)

### BackPack programs

Looking for extra help on the weekend? BackPack programs give children free, easy-to-make meals for the weekend and school breaks.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/backpack-program)

[![Irene standing in a school food pantry.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2025-11/Irene%20400X200.jpg?h=1f38fdba&itok=G_kSC9nI)

### School pantries

School pantries are partnerships between food banks and schools, making it easy for students to pick up food for their families.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/school-pantry)

[![A boy eating a meal after school.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2023-11/20220330_FeedingAmerica_SanAntonio-144-EDITED-400x200.jpg?h=4a7d1ed4&itok=g3eDcZmE)

### Kids Cafe

Kids Cafes offer free meals to kids after school, on the weekends, and during the summer. They also have fun activities and tutoring.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/kids-cafe)

[![Genie wearing a chef hat and smiling.](/sites/default/files/styles/400x200/public/2025-09/Chef%20Genie%20400X200.jpg?h=1f38fdba&itok=e4yElXPm)

### Summer meal programs

When school’s out, summer meal programs continue offering free meals for kids nationwide.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/hunger-relief-programs/summer-food-service-program)

## Find a food program in your area

Finding food assistance in your area is easy. Here are the steps.

1

Enter your zip code

We’ll share a list of Feeding America partner food banks in your area.

2

Contact your food bank

Visit their website or call to find out about the type of food assistance they offer.

3

Get your free food

Your food bank will tell you when and where food distributions happen in your area.
