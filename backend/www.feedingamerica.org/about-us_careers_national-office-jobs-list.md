# Current Job Openings at the Feeding America National Office

Our national office staff—headquartered in Chicago with an office in Washington, D.C. and remote employees across the US —number more than 250.

Thank you for your interest in a position with the Feeding America National Office. Once you have submitted your application and qualifications for a current opening, you will be notified via email to confirm receipt of your application.

A cover letter is required with your application.

We are interested in every qualified candidate who is eligible to work in the United States. However, we are not able to sponsor visas.

[View Open Positions](http://jobs.jobvite.com/feedingamerica/?nl=1)

**Beware of Recruitment Scams**  
At Feeding America National Organization, we prioritize the safety and security of our job applicants. Please be aware of fraudulent job postings and recruitment scams. All legitimate job opportunities with Feeding America are posted exclusively on our official website.  
  
**Important Tips:**

* Always verify job postings by visiting our career pages.
* Do not provide personal information to anyone outside of our official application process.
* Feeding America National Organization will never ask for financial information or require any payment of fees during the interview process or to secure employment.

Stay vigilant and protect your personal information. If you have any doubts or concerns, please [contact us directly through our website](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/contact-us).
