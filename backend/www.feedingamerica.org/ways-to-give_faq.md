# FAQs about Giving

Have questions about donating to Feeding America? Get answers to some of the questions most often asked about donating.

## Donating and donation options

### How do I donate?

* You can [donate online](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/donate) or by calling our Donor Services team at 1-800-910-5524.

  You can also [donate by mail](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/give-offline?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) by sending our [mail donation form](https://www.feedingamerica.org/sites/default/files/2022-06/PrintandSendDonationForm_2022.pdf) along with your check to:

  Feeding America  
  P.O. Box 96749  
  Washington, D.C. 20090-6749

### Will I get a receipt for my donation?

* If you make an online gift using Feeding America's website, we will automatically send a receipt to the email you used to make your donation. If you do not see a receipt for your gift in your inbox or SPAM folder, please visit the [Account Center](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center).

  If you donated by phone or mail, you'll receive an official, printed confirmation letter and receipt in the mail.

  If you need a copy of your donation receipt, don't hesitate to contact our Donor Services team at 1-800-910-5524.

### How can I make a food donation or product donation?

* Feeding America accepts a variety of [excess food and grocery donations](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/corporate-and-foundations/product-partner?s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_src=W21BREFER&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) from companies of all sizes.   However, Feeding America does not accept food donations from individual community members. If you want to [donate food](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/food-donations), we encourage you to [contact your local food bank](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) about their current needs and process.

## Donor accounts and managing your gift

### How do I log into my account?

* You can manage your giving using [your Donor Account](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center). Simply submit your email address to receive your log-in information.

  If you are a member food bank, [you can log into HungerNet](https://feedingamerica.sharepoint.com/) to manage your profile, submit a job or find event information.

### How do I cancel my monthly gift?

* We understand that circumstances change, and you may not be able to continue a monthly gift. If you donated through the mail or over the phone, please call us at 1-800-910-5524 to stop your monthly donation.

  You can cancel your monthly gift in your [Account Center](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center) if you donate online with a credit card. Click the contributions tab and edit the monthly donation you want to cancel. Next, select the cancel gift option under your payment amount and confirm that you would like to cancel your gift. Your cancellation will be effective immediately, and no further payments will be processed.

### When will my monthly gift by credit card or bank transfer be processed?

* If you signed up for monthly giving by mail or over the phone, your deduction will either be on the fourth of every month or the nineteenth of every month. If you registered to be a monthly donor online, your automatic payment will occur one month from the day you sign up.

### How do I get a copy of my tax statement or giving history?

* If you donated by December 31st, Feeding America will send you a copy of your tax statement by mail or email in February. Be sure to keep an eye on your mailbox or your junk folder. You can always view your gift history in the [Account Center](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center).

  If you need a copy or you have questions about your tax statement or giving history, please get in touch with our Donor Services team at 1-800-910-5524.

### How do I change my address or payment information?

* You can call our Donor Services team at 1-800-910-5524 to update your payment information, gift amount, or contact information. You can also use the [Account Center](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center) to update your contact information. We do not recommend you send changes or updates to your credit card by postal mail for security reasons.

### How can I request a refund for my donation?

* We are grateful for your donation and support. Your donation allows us to provide more meals to our neighbors facing hunger and support food banks and food programs in your community.

  Like many organizations, Feeding America considers all donations to be nonrefundable. However, we may consider refunds on a case-by-case basis. Please check our  [Donation Refund Policy page](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/faq/refund-policy) for more information.

## Communication preferences

### How can I be removed from the mailing list?

* Please call us at 1-800-910-5524 or [contact us online](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/contact-us) to request removal from our mailing list. You may also mail your removal request to us at:

  Feeding America  
  161 North Clark Street  
  Suite 700  
  Chicago, IL 60601

### How can I unsubscribe from the email list?

* Click the unsubscribe link at the bottom of any email we have sent, or [contact us online](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/contact-us) to request removal from our email list.

### How can I reduce the amount or frequency of the mail I receive from Feeding America?

* If you wish to reduce the amount of mail you receive from Feeding America, please call us at 1-800-910-5524 and ask to speak with Donor Services. There are various options for reduced mailing schedules, and we welcome your call to discuss these options with us.

### How long will it take before I stop receiving mail from Feeding America?

* Please allow up to 10 weeks for mail to stop because we prepare data files and print our mailings far in advance

## Where does my donation go?

### Will my donation be used wisely?

* [Feeding America invests 98% of all cash and non-cash donations into programs and services](https://www.feedingamerica.org/about-us/financials?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) that help millions of people facing hunger. This includes collecting and distributing food donations to front-line organizations like food banks, food pantries, and soup kitchens. And, only 0.3% of our total budget covers overhead and administrative costs.

  Feeding America was [recognized for its commitment to transparency, accountability, and financial responsibility](https://www.feedingamerica.org/about-us/why-feeding-america/charitable-ratings?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) by Charity Navigator, GuideStar, and Forbes.

### If I donate to Feeding America, will my donation go to my local food bank?

* Donations to Feeding America support our entire mission, including providing food and funds to local food banks. However, we cannot earmark funds for a specific location, state, or food bank.

  If you are interested in donating to your local food bank, we invite you to use our [food bank locater](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1) and select the "Give Locally" option on its profile or visit their website directly.

### How is it possible to provide at least ten meals with just one dollar?

* Every dollar donated to Feeding America allows us to secure and distribute at least 12 pounds of food (10 average-sized meals). We rescue food from manufacturers, retailers, and growers on behalf of local food banks. Your donations help us secure and transport that food and pay the staff who make it all possible.

  [Read more about the impact of dollars donated to Feeding America](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/faq/about-our-claims?s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1&s_src=W21BREFER&s_referrer=https%253A%252F%252Fwww.feedingamerica.org%252F&s_subsrc=https%253A%252F%252Fsecure.feedingamerica.org%252Fsite%252FDonation2%253Fdf_id%253D29950%252629950.donation%253Dform1).
