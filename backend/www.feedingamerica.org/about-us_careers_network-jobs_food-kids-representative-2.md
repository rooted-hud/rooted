# Food For Kids Representative

Food Bank

[Food Bank of the Rockies](https://www.feedingamerica.org/find-your-local-foodbank/food-bank-of-the-rockies)

City

Aurora

State

Colorado

Post date

March 27, 2026

Description

The Food for Kids (FFK) Representative assists with overall operational and logistical functions related to Food Bank of the Rockies’ kids’ meals programs: Child & Adult Care Food Program (CACFP), Summer Food Service Program (SFSP), and Totes of Hope®. It works collaboratively with the Kitchen, Transportation, and Operations teams to organize and fulfill meal service and programmatic needs.

This position is instrumental to advance the work of Food Bank of the Rockies to connect with Hunger Relief Partners to forge community-based, neighbor-centered hunger relief systems that both nourish those experiencing hunger and address the root causes of hunger.

End date

April 03, 2026

Opportunity type

Job

Category

Programs / Agency Relations / Clients

Job responsibilities

Operations:  
• Responsible for maintaining and strengthening relationships with all CACFP/SFSP/Totes sites, providing excellent customer service through email, phone, and in-person visits  
• Work in collaboration with Food Bank of the Rockies' Kitchen, Operations, Sourcing, and Transportation teams, and outside meal vendors to ensure timely and detailed communication of program needs and procedures for new and existing sites  
• Work with Nutrition Manager as needed to ensure meals or product offered include a variety of HER ratings when possible and follow any compliance requirements  
• Perform site monitor duties, develop and maintain site visit schedules for reviews and visits, and complete all the required documentation to ensure compliance with CACFP/SFSP/Totes  
• Assist FFK Supervisor in collecting and organizing data and records by processing required paperwork for program including applications and site visits to ensure accuracy and completeness. Reporting any findings to Supervisor

People-Focused Commitment:  
• Conduct work with a culture of respect for all people and experiences  
• Committed to a learning mindset and continuous improvement regarding practice of inclusion in all aspects of work  
• Performs both internal and external work in a way that is equitably inclusive and effective within our diverse communities  
• Regularly assesses work and supports changes to remove barriers, build systems that address inequities and integrates equity and inclusiveness into all areas of work.  
• Handle confidential information with discretion and care, ensuring the privacy and respect of employees, volunteers, partners, donors, or others

Application qualifications

Required Knowledge, Skills, and Abilities:  
• Commitment to fulfilling Food Bank of the Rockies mission and positively representing the brand both internally and externally  
• Demonstrates compassion and sensitivity  
• Intermediate to Advanced Computer Proficiency (Word, Excel, and Outlook)  
• Excellent organizational, time management, and follow-through skills  
• Self-directed and results oriented  
• Proactive and independent problem-solver  
• Exercises independent judgement, think critically and strategically, collaborate with others, and follow through to execute projects and program requirements  
• Strong customer service orientation  
• Ability to communicate persuasively and articulately both verbally and in writing  
• Keep abreast of regulations updates and developments in FFK programs

Required Qualifications:  
• High school diploma or equivalent required  
• Previous customer service experience  
• Extensive experience using Microsoft Office Suite and online technologies required  
• Experience using collaborative tools for file storage and sharing, meeting scheduling, and virtual collaboration preferred  
• Must have a valid driver’s license

About the organization

As the food bank with the largest service area in the contiguous United States, Food Bank of the Rockies is passionate about ensuring every community member has the resources they need to thrive. Supporting our Colorado and Wyoming communities begins with passionate, mission-driven individuals.

The Food Bank’s team is dedicated to creating an efficient food distribution network to reduce and help end hunger across the state. Staff are committed to embodying the Food Bank’s organizational values and mission in all activities.

If you are looking for a mission-focused organization where innovation and teamwork are encouraged and new ideas are valued, then this is an ideal position for you.

The mission of Food Bank of the Rockies is to ignite the power of community to nourish people facing hunger. We provide food and necessities to people in need through signature programs and by teaming up with hundreds of Hunger Relief Partners to serve communities across Colorado and Wyoming. We believe that for a community to thrive, every person must have the resources they need to flourish, and we strive to provide proper nourishment for every neighbor.
