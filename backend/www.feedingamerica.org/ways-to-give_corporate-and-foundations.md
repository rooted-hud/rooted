# How to become a corporate partner

We are so grateful for your interest in supporting Feeding America.

Our corporate donors and partners provide significant resources that allow Feeding America to achieve our vision of a hunger-free America.

## Ways to partner with Feeding America

Committed corporate and foundation partners help the Feeding America network reach over 40 million people each year. Their generous financial support, product donations, and volunteers make a measurable impact in the fight to end hunger in their communities and throughout the country.
