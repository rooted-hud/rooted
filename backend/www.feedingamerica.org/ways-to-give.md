## Frequently Asked Questions About Donating

### How much does my donation help?

* Every $1 donated helps provide at least 10 meals through the Feeding America nationwide network of food banks. We get food to individuals and families where it's needed most.

### Is my donation tax-deductible?

* Yes, Feeding America is a [501(c)(3) non-profit organization](https://www.feedingamerica.org/ways-to-give/faq/about-our-claims), and your donation is tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a donation receipt for your records.

### What percentage of my donation goes directly to programs?

* We use [98% of all donations](https://www.feedingamerica.org/about-us/financials)—cash and non-cash—directly for programs that feed people facing hunger.

### Can I designate my donation to a specific area?

* Donations are used where they're most needed across our programs nationwide. For specific disaster relief, you can donate at feedingamerica.org/disaster.

### How will I receive confirmation of my donation?

* You will receive an email confirmation immediately after making your online donation. For mail-in donations, a receipt will be sent to the address provided.

### How can I change or cancel my monthly donation?

* [Log into your account](https://give.feedingamerica.org/a/my-account-center) to easily manage your recurring donations. If you donated via mail or phone, contact our Donor Services team at [1-800-910-5524](tel:1-800-910-5524).
