# About Feeding America

Feeding America is a nationwide network of food banks, food pantries and local meal programs. Our common goal is simple: help people get the food and resources they need to thrive.

Everything we do focuses on getting nutritious food to communities – from sourcing food donations to advocating for policies that end hunger. [Learn more about how we’re working to end hunger.](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work)

## [Our Leadership](https://www.feedingamerica.org/about-us/leadership)
