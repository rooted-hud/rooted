# Disaster Relief Partners

When a disaster strikes, widespread devastation leaves communities without food, power, and shelter. Feeding America's disaster-relief partners allow member food banks to provide [needed food assistance and emergency supplies](https://www.feedingamerica.org/our-work/disaster-response "How We Respond to Disasters") to affected areas in the immediate aftermath and throughout the recovery process. Thank you to our partners for supporting the Feeding America food bank network's response to [Hurricane Helene](https://www.feedingamerica.org/hunger-blog/how-are-food-banks-and-feeding-america-responding-hurricane-helene) and [Hurricane Milton](https://www.feedingamerica.org/hunger-blog/feeding-america-responds-hurricane-milton) and year-round disaster efforts.
